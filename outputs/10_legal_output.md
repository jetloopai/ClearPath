## 5. Email / SMS Compliance

### CAN-SPAM (Email)

| Requirement | Implementation |
|---|---|
| Identify as an ad | Marketing emails labeled appropriately |
| Include physical address | Footer of every email |
| Include unsubscribe link | Every email (Resend handles this) |
| Honor opt-outs within 10 days | Automated via Resend/MailerLite |
| No deceptive subject lines | Internal review before sending |

### TCPA (SMS)

| Requirement | Implementation |
|---|---|
| Express written consent | Checkbox on Asset Group contact form: "I consent to receive text messages from ClearPath" |
| Opt-out mechanism | Reply STOP at any time |
| Identify sender | Every SMS includes "- ClearPath" |
| No messages before 8am or after 9pm | Twilio send windows configured |
| Record of consent | Stored in `leads.tags` array + `lead_activity` log |

## 6. Fair Housing Compliance (Lease-Up)

ClearPath must comply with:

| Law | Scope |
|---|---|
| **Federal Fair Housing Act** | Cannot discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, familial status, disability |
| **Illinois Human Rights Act** | Adds: ancestry, age, marital status, sexual orientation, military status |
| **Cook County Human Rights Ordinance** | Adds: source of income (Section 8 / housing vouchers must be accepted) |

**Internal Rules:**
- Tenant screening criteria are objective and applied uniformly
- No steering, preferencing, or discouraging any protected class
- All listings include Equal Housing Opportunity logo
- Document every screening decision with reason codes

## 7. Business Entity Recommendation

| Structure | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| **ClearPath Analyzer** (the tool) | LLC — shields personal assets from software liability |
| **ClearPath Asset Group** (the service) | Separate LLC — isolates rehab/service liability from the tech product |

> **Recommended:** Form two LLCs under one parent entity or keep them as two standalone LLCs. Consult an Illinois business attorney.

**Registrations Needed:**
- Illinois LLC formation (Secretary of State)
- EIN from IRS
- Cook County business license
- Illinois Department of Revenue (if collecting fees)
- General liability insurance ($1M minimum recommended)
- Professional liability / E&O insurance (for the service)
