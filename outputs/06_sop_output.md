## Overview

This document defines the standard operating procedure for the ClearPath Asset Group — the execution arm that takes qualified investors from "I have a deal" to "I have a performing asset." All operations are within **Cook County, IL** (Chicago + suburbs).

**Three Core Services:**

| Service | What We Do | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| **Rehab Management** | Scope, permit, execute, and close out renovation | 6–16 weeks |
| **Lease-Up** | Market, screen, and place tenants | 2–4 weeks |
| **Stabilization** | Hand off a cash-flowing, tenant-occupied property | 1–2 weeks post-lease |

**End State:** Client receives a stabilized, rent-producing asset with all documentation.

## Phase 1: Discovery & Qualification

### Discovery Call (30 min)

**Objective:** Determine if the deal is viable and the client is a fit.

**Checklist:**

| Item | Question / Action |
|---|---|
| Property address | Confirm it's in Cook County |
| Purchase status | Under contract? Closed? Still looking? |
| Budget | What's their total rehab budget? |
| Strategy | Flip or hold? |
| Timeline | When do they need it done? |
| Financing | Cash, conventional, DSCR, hard money? |
| Experience level | First deal or experienced? |
| Expectations | What does "done" look like to them? |

**Outcome:**
- ✅ **Proceed** → Schedule property walkthrough
- ❌ **Decline** → Send "not a fit" email with reason + referral if possible
- ⏸️ **Hold** → Client not ready yet → Move to `nurture` in CRM

---

### Property Assessment (On-Site)

**Who:** Project manager or lead contractor
**When:** Within 5 business days of discovery call
**Duration:** 1–2 hours

**Assessment Checklist:**

| Category | Items to Inspect |
|---|---|
| **Structural** | Foundation, load-bearing walls, floors |
| **Roof** | Age, condition, leaks, gutters |
| **HVAC** | Furnace age, AC, ductwork |
| **Plumbing** | Supply lines (copper/PEX/galvanized), drain condition, water heater |
| **Electrical** | Panel amperage, knob & tube?, GFCI in wet areas |
| **Kitchen** | Layout, cabinets, counters, appliance plan |
| **Bathrooms** | Vanity, tile, tub/shower condition |
| **Cosmetic** | Paint, flooring, trim, fixtures |
| **Exterior** | Siding, windows, landscaping, garage |
| **Code / Permits** | Any open violations? Chicago dept. of buildings check |

**Output:** Written assessment with photos → feeds into Scope of Work.

**Before any work begins, the client must sign:**

| Document | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Service Agreement** | Defines scope, timeline, payment terms, ClearPath responsibilities |
| **SOW Approval** | Client signs off on specific line items and budget |

| Agreement signed | 25% of total budget |

| 75% completion (drywall, flooring, kitchen) | 25% |

### Inspection Milestones (Cook County / City of Chicago)

| Inspection | When | Who Calls |
|---|---|---|
| Permit pulled | Before work starts | ClearPath |
| Rough-in (electrical) | After wiring, before drywall | Electrician |
| Rough-in (plumbing) | After rough, before drywall | Plumber |
| Framing (if structural) | After framing, before drywall | ClearPath |
| Final building inspection | After all work complete | ClearPath |
| Certificate of occupancy (if applicable) | After final inspection | City |

---

### Punch List Process

**After the rehab is "done" — before calling it complete:**

1. PM does a full walk-through with the **ClearPath Punch List Checklist**
2. All items documented with photos
3. Contractors return to fix all items (no additional charge)
4. PM does a second walk-through to confirm
5. **Client final walk** — any remaining items noted and resolved
6. Client signs **Rehab Completion Acknowledgment**

## Phase 4: Lease-Up

### Marketing the Property

**Timeline:** Listing goes live within **3 business days** of rehab completion.

| Channel | Action |
|---|---|
| Zillow / Trulia / HotPads | Syndicated listing via Zillow Rental Manager |
| Facebook Marketplace | Posted with photos + key details |
| Craigslist | Posted in Chicago housing section |
| Yard sign | Installed at property |
| ClearPath network | Emailed to local agent/PM contacts |

**Listing Requirements:**
- Minimum 15 professional photos
- Floor plan (if available)
- Rent price (from ClearPath Analyzer or market research)
- Key features listed (updated kitchen, new HVAC, etc.)
- Application link included in listing

---

### Tenant Screening Criteria

| Criterion | Minimum Standard |
|---|---|
| Credit score | 600+ (flexible with strong income) |
| Income | 3× monthly rent (gross) |
| Rental history | 2+ years, no evictions |
| Criminal background | No violent felonies in past 7 years |
| Employment | Verified current employment or proof of income |
| References | 1 previous landlord + 1 personal |

> **Compliance:** All screening follows Fair Housing Act + Cook County Human Rights Ordinance. No discrimination based on source of income (Section 8 accepted per Cook County law).

---

### Lease Execution

| Step | Action | Who |
|---|---|---|
| Application received | Screen tenant within 48 hours | ClearPath |
| Tenant approved | Send lease for signature | ClearPath |
| Lease signed | Collect security deposit + first month | ClearPath |
| Move-in inspection | Document condition with photos + checklist | ClearPath |
| Keys handed over | Tenant moves in | ClearPath |

**Lease Terms (Standard):**
- 12-month lease
- Security deposit = 1 month rent
- Late fee = $50 after 5-day grace period
- Tenant responsible for utilities (gas, electric, water if separately metered)

## Phase 5: Stabilization & Handoff

### Stabilization Package

Once the tenant is placed and rent is flowing, ClearPath delivers the **Stabilization Package** to the client:

| Document | Contents |
|---|---|
| **Final Rehab Report** | SOW vs. actuals, total spend, before/after photos |
| **Executed Lease** | Signed copy of tenant lease |
| **Tenant Profile** | Name, contact, employer, income verification |
| **Move-In Inspection** | Photo documentation of property condition at move-in |
| **Warranty Info** | Any active warranties (appliances, HVAC, roof, etc.) |
| **Contractor Contacts** | List of contractors used + their specialties |
| **Property Tax Info** | Current tax bill + appeal status if applicable |
| **Insurance Recommendation** | Landlord policy quote or current policy details |
| **Cash Flow Projection** | Updated cash flow based on actual rent + expenses |

---

### Property Management Handoff

**Option A: Client self-manages**
- ClearPath provides a "Self-Management Starter Kit" (lease enforcement guide, maintenance contacts, rent collection setup via Rentec/Apartments.com)

**Option B: ClearPath refers to PM company**
- Warm handoff to a vetted Cook County property management company
- ClearPath provides all stabilization docs to new PM

**Option C: ClearPath manages (Phase 3 expansion)**
- Ongoing PM at 8–10% of monthly rent
- Not available in MVP — future service line

## Guardrails

- **Never start work without a signed agreement and first draw payment**
- **Never exceed budget without written client approval (change order)**
- **Never skip inspections** — Cook County violations can kill a deal
- **Never place a tenant without full screening** — evictions destroy cash flow
- **Always document everything** — photos, receipts, communications
