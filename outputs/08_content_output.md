## Overview

The content engine exists for one reason: **drive traffic to the Analyzer tool.** Every piece of content should end with a version of "Run the numbers yourself → clearpath.com."

The Analyzer does the selling. Content does the attracting.

**Content Strategy in One Line:**
> Teach people how to analyze deals → Give them a free tool to do it → Capture their email → If they're in Cook County, pitch the service.

## Content Formats (Templates)

### Format 1: Deal Breakdown Video (YouTube — 8–12 min)

**Structure:**
```
[Hook — 15 sec]
"I just found a 3-bed in Tinley Park listed at $115K. 
Let me show you if the numbers actually work."

[The Property — 1 min]
Show listing photos, map, neighborhood context.

[Run the Analyzer — 3 min]
Screen record of entering the address into ClearPath.
Walk through each output: ARV, rehab, rent, cash flow, flip profit.

[The Verdict — 2 min]
Is it a deal? Green, yellow, or red? Why?
What would the MAO be?

[The Lesson — 2 min]
One takeaway the viewer can apply to their own deals.
"Always check the rehab-to-ARV ratio..."

[CTA — 30 sec]
"Want to run your own deal? Link in the description. 
It's free. Takes 60 seconds."
```

**Output:** 1 long video → 3–4 short clips → 1 carousel → 2 tweets

### Format 3: Short-Form Video (Reels / Shorts / TikTok — 30–60 sec)

**Hook Types That Work:**

| Hook | Example |
|---|---|
| The "I Found" Hook | "I found a house for $80K that cash flows $400/month" |
| The Myth Buster | "Everyone says you need 20% down. Wrong." |
| The Quick Tip | "One number that tells you if a deal is worth it" |
| The Controversy | "Stop buying rentals in these zip codes" |
| The Behind the Scenes | "Day 1 of our gut rehab in Oak Forest" |

**Structure:**
```
[Hook — 3 sec]  → Stop the scroll
[Setup — 10 sec] → Context
[Value — 30 sec] → The lesson or walkthrough
[CTA — 5 sec]   → "Link in bio to analyze your own deals"
```

→ clearpath.com"

## Repurposing System

One long-form piece produces 6+ pieces of content:

```
1 YouTube Video (10 min)
    ├── 3 Short clips (Reels / Shorts / TikTok)
    ├── 1 Instagram carousel (key screenshots + numbers)
    ├── 1 Twitter thread (text version of the breakdown)
    ├── 1 Newsletter feature (Deal of the Week)
    └── 1 Blog post (SEO-optimized written version)
```

**Rule:** Never create content that only lives in one place.
