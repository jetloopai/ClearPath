## Usage Notes

- **Indigo `#6366f1`** is the primary brand color — used for CTAs, active states, focused inputs, selection highlights, and background glows.
- **Violet** is a secondary accent used exclusively for the Pro pricing tier.
- **Emerald / Amber / Red** are reserved for deal signal indicators only — do not repurpose them for UI chrome.
- The app uses a near-black background (`#050505`) with a radial indigo-tinted gradient at the top (`#0a0a1a`), giving depth without being pure black.
- Glass panels use `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.03)` backgrounds with a subtle `backdrop-filter: blur(16px)`.
